morning light in our living room

last experienced: 02.22.2021

I am a morning person. And when the sun rises in the morning, it often wakes me up. But I wake up happy when I see the sun because it looks so beautiful in our living room. The sun shines right into the window from the east and casts a large block of sunshine onto the opposing wall. I like to sip my tea and sit in the sunlight to feel its warmth while it lasts.
