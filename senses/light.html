flickering light bulb

last experienced: 02.12.2021

Recently the light in our hallway of our apartment building has been flickering. Our door is at the end of the hallway, so it creates a very ominous feeling. It’s strange because I love returning home to see my roommate and be in the comfort of my own space, but when I see this light above our door, there’s a little narrative forming in my head that I may be entering a haunted space.
